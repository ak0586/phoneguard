Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2026

PhoneGuard ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the PhoneGuard mobile application (the "App") and its associated services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide effective lost phone recovery and security services, we collect the following types of information:

A. Personal Information

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and mobile number provided during registration.
  • Trusted Contacts: Mobile numbers you manually provide to receive security alerts.

B. Sensitive Device Data

  • Location Data: We collect precise and coarse location data, including in the background, to allow you to track your device if it is lost or stolen.
  • Camera Images: The App captures front-camera selfies only when a failed unlock attempt is detected (Intrusion Detection) or when you remotely trigger a "Capture" command.
  • SMS Data: The App monitors incoming SMS messages solely to identify and execute your authorized "Trigger Keyword" and commands (e.g., alarm, track, lock). We do not read or store personal messages.

C. Technical Data

  • Device Metadata: Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers to ensure commands are delivered to the correct hardware.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data exclusively for security purposes:

  • Device Recovery: To send location coordinates to your trusted contacts.
  • Evidence Collection: To store intruder photos in your private cloud storage for identification.
  • Service Execution: To remotely trigger alarms, lock screens, or track devices via SMS.
  • Communication: To send security alerts and account-related notifications.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share information with service providers only as necessary:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, real-time database synchronization, and secure photo storage.
  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements in the free version of the App.
  • Firebase Crashlytics & Analytics: To monitor App performance and improve user experience.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Retention: We retain your data only as long as your account is active.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data (including stored photos and location history) at any time through the "Settings" or "Profile" section of the App.

5. Permissions and Hardware Access

The App requires specific Android permissions to function as a security utility:

  • Background Location: Necessary to track and recover the device even when the App is closed or in the background.
  • SMS Receiver: Necessary to identify and execute remote recovery commands without an internet connection.
  • Camera: Necessary for Intrusion Detection selfies and remote capture commands.
  • Contacts: Used solely to allow you to select "Trusted Numbers" from your existing phonebook. We do not store, upload, or share your contact list.
  • Device Administrator: Necessary to allow you to remotely lock your screen from another device.
  • Phone State: Necessary to detect SIM card changes and notify your trusted contacts.

We implement robust security measures, including industry-standard encryption for all data transmitted to our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the App.
